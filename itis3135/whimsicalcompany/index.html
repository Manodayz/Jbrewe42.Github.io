Introducing the Joyous Bee Heat Pad — a revolutionary, eco-conscious solution that turns your laptop’s wasted heat into usable energy. Using cutting-edge HTEG (High-Temperature Energy Generation) technology, this pad discreetly fits under your computer and captures its byproduct warmth to wirelessly charge your phone or other small devices.

Perfect for students, professionals, or tech lovers looking to minimize waste and stay powered up — without needing an extra outlet.

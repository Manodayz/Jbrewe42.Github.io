About Joyous Bee Designs

Joyous Bee Designs is the company behind the innovative Joyous Bee Heat Pad — a sleek, flexible energy recycler that uses your laptop’s heat to wirelessly charge devices. This creative concept took shape in 2023, when a team of four students at Nesbitt Discovery Academy teamed up to solve a simple but frustrating problem: all the wasted heat pouring out of laptops, especially during long study sessions.

The Joyous Bee pad features a collection of 12 HTEG (High-Temperature Energy Generators) embedded in a bendable silicon mat. These are connected in series to maximize energy conversion. The underside is equipped with heat sinks to stabilize the device, while the top layer is finished with a beautiful and functional copper-toned Faraday Fabric — giving it both a techy aesthetic and EM shielding to reduce interference.

The prototype began as a classroom project, but its clean energy potential and sleek design caught attention. Now, Joyous Bee Designs is on a mission to make the Joyous Bee pad a common tool in every laptop user’s arsenal.

Other applications include:

  • Cooling down hot kitchen appliances
  • Cooling down or heating up food (if providing power instead of generating)
  • Generating electricity by being a coaster on a table for hot food

And much, much more!

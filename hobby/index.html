What is Dungeons and Dragons?

Dungeons and Dragons (DnD) is a fantasy role-playing game where players create characters and embark on adventures guided by a Dungeon Master, which is what I do in my party.

Our campaign takes place in the Eberron world, a unique magepunk setting full of magic, political intrigue, and chaos.

If you don't know what Magepunk is, I'd suggest watching "Arcane". Very good show and a great example of a magepunk world (past episode 3).

When Do We Play?

We meet every Sunday at 6PM EST for our weekly sessions. It's a great way to unwind and enjoy a shared adventure.

Our sessions typically last for 3-4 hours, depending on how the story progresses.

Image source: Jesse Brewer

Where Do We Play?

We play in a private Discord server, where we have set up channels for our game, voice chat, and general communication.

It's convenient and allows for easy sharing of game materials and quick communication between sessions.

For transferring of data on homebrewed material, we have a shared google drive folder to share our own creations.

Image source: discord.com/branding

Why Do We Play?

DnD is a source of personal enjoyment, relaxation, and creativity. It allows us to escape into a world of fantasy and storytelling.

It also helps us develop teamwork, problem-solving, and communication skills while having fun together.

How Do We Play?

We use 5etools, an open-source downloadable catalogue of information for DnD. It helps us access character sheets, spells, monsters, and more.

5etools is an excellent resource for both new and experienced players, providing all the information we need for a seamless experience.
